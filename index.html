"Shaping A Better Tomorrow Through Intensive and Integrated Education"

Keeping the dream to have a well-knowledged and multi-skilled Islamic Scholars in mind, the visionaries behind MARKHINS started Markaz Khykha Institute of Higher Islamic Studies (MARKHINS) at Ulsoor (near MG Road), Bangalore in the year 2010. At MARKHINS, students can stay and experience the knowledge-imparting concept of Islam in its real meaning by growing as students and transforming themselves into spiritual and social trend-setters of a new generation. By the Grace of Almighty Allah, MARKHINS I is commencing its successful journey into its eighth year with around 150 students.

  • Mission

    “To produce spiritually enlightened and professionally skilled group of Islamic Scholars who will become Change Agents of the Muslim Umma in order to enable them to meet the challenges of tomorrow with traditional values and global exposure.”



Special Features

  • The first Islamic Institute having a combined course of Islamic and academic Studies Concurrently in India.
  • Highly sophisticated smart classrooms and IT Lab
  • Well equipped Library.
  • Certificate course in professional online Da’wa (CPOD)
  • 150 Hours of compulsory Islamic project work in practical Da’wa.
  • Khykha Composite PU College affiliated with Karnataka PUE Board
  • Khykha College of Commerce (B.Com and M.com of Bangalore University)
